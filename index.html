◆ DISCOVERY Understand why they are really here · Not the surface answer · The real thing
OPENING
FOLLOW UP WITH"What made you decide to jump on the call today?"
What got you interested in this?
How long has this been on your mind?
What are you hoping this could do for you?
What made now the right time?
Have you been looking into this for a while?
ABOUT THEM
FOLLOW UP WITH"Tell me a bit about yourself."
Tell me a bit about life outside work.
What do you do day to day?
How long have you been doing that?
What does a normal week look like for you?
POWER QUESTIONS
FOLLOW UP WITH"[Their word]?" · "What do you mean by [their word]?"
Tell me more about that.
Say more about that.
What do you mean by that?
Can you expand on that?
Walk me through that.
Help me understand that.
Earlier you mentioned ________ — what did you mean by that?
CURRENT SITUATION
FOLLOW UP WITH"What's happening right now?"
What's not working?
What's stopping you?
How long has this been happening?
What frustrates you most?
What's the thing that's sitting heaviest on you?
◆ PAIN Find current pain · Current frustration · Current dissatisfaction · Get emotional truth
PAIN — SURFACE
FOLLOW UP WITH"What's the hardest part about that?"
What's frustrating about that?
What impact is that having on your life?
What's the real cost of staying where you are?
What's that costing you?
What's the most frustrating part of all this?
◆ IN DEPTH — TTT Stay here · This is where most beginners fail · Time · Tried · Consequence
T — TIME
FOLLOW UP WITH"How long have you been dealing with this?"
How many years have you been living with this?
When did you first realise this was becoming a problem?
You've already been dealing with this for [X] years — could you realistically do another [X]?
What would another 5 years of this look like?
How much longer are you willing to tolerate this?
T — TRIED
FOLLOW UP WITH"What have you tried so far?"
What happened?
Why didn't that work?
What was missing from that?
Have you worked with anyone before?
What's stopping you from figuring this out on your own?
T — CONSEQUENCE
FOLLOW UP WITH"What happens if nothing changes?"
What happens if you're still here next year?
How would you feel if nothing changed?
What's the cost of staying here?
How much longer can you realistically keep doing this?
At what point does staying where you are become harder than making a change?
What's the hardest part about knowing you're capable of more but still stuck in this cycle?
If we're having this exact same conversation a year from now — what does that look like?
◆ DREAM New Vision · Move them forward · Get emotionally attached to a better future
NEW VISION
FOLLOW UP WITH"If this worked perfectly — what would change first?"
What would life look like if this problem disappeared?
What would become possible?
What would a great day look like?
What would having that freedom mean to you?
What would you be doing more of?
MAKE IT PERSONAL
FOLLOW UP WITH"What would your family notice?"
How would your confidence change?
Who else would benefit if you got this right?
What would getting this right mean for you personally?
What excites you most about that future?
MAKE IT REAL
FOLLOW UP WITH"If we were having this conversation 12 months from now and everything had gone perfectly — what would you be telling me?"
How different would life feel if that became reality?
What would that mean for you and your family?
What would change first?
What's the part of that future that excites you most?
◆ KINDRED — TRUST Discover why they already trust you · Get them telling you why they chose you
KINDRED
FOLLOW UP WITH"What made you reach out to us?"
What stood out?
Why this instead of trying to figure it out yourself?
Why this instead of another programme?
What is it about this that feels right?
◆ ATM — QUALIFYING Authority · Time · Money · Handle now or handle as objections later
A — AUTHORITY
FOLLOW UP WITH"Tell me a bit about yourself outside of work."
Married, in a relationship, or flying solo these days?
How long have you guys been together?
Got any kids?
Does she know you're on this call — or are we on a secret mission?
When you make bigger decisions like this — do you take the lead or talk it through together?
If today felt right — is that a call you could make yourself?
T — TIME COMMITMENT
FOLLOW UP WITH"If this takes a few hours a week — could you realistically make that happen?"
What would need to change for you to make time?
If this was important enough — could you make time for it?
You've been dealing with this for [X] years. If you can't make it a priority now — when will you ever?
If nothing changes, what happens?
M — MONEY
FOLLOW UP WITH"Have you invested in yourself before?"
What was that experience like?
What did you like? What didn't you like?
What was missing?
What would you want differently this time?
M — WILLINGNESS
FOLLOW UP WITH"Is money actually the thing that would stop you — or is it more making sure you're making the right decision?"
If 1 is money would be a genuine concern and 10 is money wouldn't stop you — where would you put yourself?
Why that number and not lower?
What would need to happen for that number to be higher?
Is that a money thing or more of a certainty thing?
Do you see this as an expense or an investment?
COMMITMENT
FOLLOW UP WITH"Be honest with me — would you find a way or would you find a reason?"
What makes you say that?
How serious are you about solving this?
Why now?
Why has this become important?
◆ SUMMARY Repeat everything back · Build trust · Create certainty · Most closers skip this
SUMMARY
FOLLOW UP WITH"Bob — just so I make sure I've understood correctly..."
Summarise their pain in their exact words.
Summarise their frustrations.
Summarise their failed attempts.
Summarise their dream and goals.
Summarise the consequences of doing nothing.
End with: "Have I got that right?"
Then: "Based on everything you've told me — I genuinely think we can help. Would you like me to walk you through how?"
